How to Start the game:
  from the capturetheflag directory do the command:
    ruby src/main.rb

How To Play:
  Be the first player to grab the flag.
  Tag three of the four corners with the flag.
  Grab the flag by standing on top of it.
  There is no time limit.

Controls:
  Press each key one time to move one tile

  Player 1:
  WASD for up, left, down, right

  Player2:
  up, left, down, and right arrow keys
